Data Deletion Request

To delete your data from Adam and Eve Store App:

Immediate Deletion:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Click Settings & Privacy
  3. Select Settings
  4. Click Apps and Websites
  5. Find "Adam and Eve Store App"
  6. Click Remove to delete all data and revoke access

Alternative Method:

Send deletion request to: adameve.store.app@gmail.com

We will process your request within 14 days as per Facebook's requirements.

This deletion mechanism complies with Facebook Platform Terms and Developer Policies.
