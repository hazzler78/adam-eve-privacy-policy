Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 15, 2024

How to Delete Your Data

The Adam and Eve Store Facebook App is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store any personal data on our servers. However, we provide multiple ways to remove your data and revoke access:

Option 1: Remove App Access through Facebook

  1. Go to Facebook Settings & Privacy
  2. Click on "Settings"
  3. Select "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find "Adam and Eve Store App"
  5. Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions and delete associated data

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

You can also request data deletion by:

  1. Sending a message to our Facebook Page
  2. Including your Facebook ID and request for data deletion
  3. We will process your request within 14 days

What Gets Deleted

When you request deletion:

  • All app permissions are revoked
  • Access to your Facebook Page is removed
  • Any cached data is cleared

Additional Information

Please note that:

  • Posts made through our app will remain on your Facebook Page unless you manually delete them
  • Facebook's own data retention policies may apply to certain information
  • You can always reinstall the app later if needed

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us through our Facebook Page.
