Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the Adam and Eve Store Facebook App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Facebook application.

Information We Collect

When you use our application, we collect:

  • Basic Facebook profile information (name and email)
  • Facebook Page management access for posting content
  • Page permissions necessary for posting updates

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Enable posting to your Facebook Page
  • Manage your Facebook Page content
  • Provide customer support

Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties. Your data is only used for the functionality of posting to your Facebook Page.

Data Storage

We do not store any of your Facebook data on our servers. We only use the necessary permissions to enable posting functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
